Prompt governance is the framework used to control how AI prompts are designed, approved, tested, versioned, and monitored. It is especially essential in investor services and transfer agency, where AI-generated outputs may involve sensitive shareholder data, regulatory reporting, transaction processing, and client communications. Strong prompt governance helps reduce errors, prevent inappropriate data exposure, maintain consistency, and ensure that AI responses comply with legal, operational, and audit requirements. It also creates clear accountability, making AI use safer, more transparent, and more reliable across critical financial-service processes.
