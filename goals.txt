

During the first half of the year, Rana has invested a considerable amount of time creating a framework for AI adoption and rollout across both the technical and business communities within 2S. His most significant contributions have included designing and launching the AI Champions programme, developing structured use case templates, coaching AI Champions on identifying and documenting opportunities, providing extensive AI training and support to the development teams, and acting as the HFS representative within the central AI governance and implementation process.

Rana has made a significant contribution in helping to demystify AI across HFS, ensuring discussions remain focused on practical business outcomes and the capabilities available within the bank, while also keeping the organisation informed about external AI developments such as ChatGPT, Claude and other leading models. His enthusiasm and willingness to support colleagues has helped build momentum for AI adoption and increased engagement across both business and technology teams.

For the second half of the year, I would like Rana to focus on evolving the AI programme from awareness and enablement towards delivering measurable business value. In particular, I would like him to:

### Key Objectives for H2

**1. Mature the AI Use Case Pipeline**

* Review and prioritise all submitted AI use cases using agreed business and technical criteria.
* Establish a governance process to track use cases from ideation through implementation.
* Identify and sponsor at least **3-5 high-value Proof of Concept (PoC)** initiatives.

**2. Deliver an HFS AI Solution Roadmap**

* Define the requirements for a centralised HFS AI platform or toolkit.
* Produce a solution architecture and implementation roadmap.
* Work with Architecture, Security and Development teams to prepare at least **one solution for production readiness**.

**3. Continue AI Education and Adoption**

* Deliver **15-20 minute AI learning sessions** during each fortnightly AI Champions meeting.
* Develop training covering topics such as Agentic AI, prompt engineering, Claude, Mistral and emerging AI capabilities.
* Create a reusable knowledge repository containing best practices, templates and learning materials.

**4. Create Practical AI Assets**

* Develop a library of **20-30 reusable prompts** and quick-win AI solutions targeted at common HFS activities.
* Publish standard templates and guidance to improve consistency and encourage wider adoption.
* Measure adoption through AI Champion engagement and user feedback.

**5. Strengthen AI Community Engagement**

* Continue mentoring AI Champions and establish regular feedback sessions.
* Increase collaboration between business and technology teams to ensure use cases solve real business problems.
* Share success stories and lessons learned to encourage further innovation.

### Personal Development

Rana has developed strong technical credibility and has become a trusted advisor on AI within HFS. Looking ahead, I would like him to continue building confidence in presenting to wider business audiences and taking ownership of discussions that extend beyond technical implementation into business transformation.

Specifically, I encourage Rana to:

* Lead AI demonstrations and presentations to senior stakeholders.
* Facilitate AI workshops with business teams.
* Take greater ownership of solution design discussions that balance both business and technical perspectives.
* Continue developing his leadership presence as one of the key AI advocates within HFS.

I believe Rana has the capability to become a recognised AI leader within the organisation. The next stage of his development is to build on the excellent foundations he has established and focus on delivering measurable business outcomes, scaling adoption, and influencing strategic AI direction across HFS.
